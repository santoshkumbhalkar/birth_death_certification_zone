About Us

Welcome to the Birth AND Death Certification Zone, your trusted one-stop platform for managing Birth and Death Certificates in Maharashtra. We understand that official documentation is vital, and our goal is to simplify and streamline the process of obtaining and updating these essential records.

Apply for Birth & Death Certificates

Effortlessly apply for official Birth and Death certificates through our online application system. No need to visit government offices; everything can be done online from the comfort of your home.

Update Certificates

Need to make changes to existing Birth or Death records? Our platform allows you to update the details quickly and accurately, ensuring your information is always up-to-date.

Generate Certificates

Once application is processed or records are updated, you can generate your official certificates directly from our platform. Simply enter your application or certificate number to retrieve your document instantly.

Why Choose Us?

We are committed to providing a user-friendly, secure, and efficient system for managing these crucial life events. Whether it's registering a new birth, updating details, or accessing historical records, our goal is to reduce the complexity and make it a hassle-free experience for every user.

Steps to Generate Certificate

  1. Open the Application of Government of Maharashtra(Health Department).
  2. Login as Admin,With fixed Username and Password
  3. After Login, Dashboard will display.
  4. Go to Application Tab and choose and fill the Birth or Death Application form.
  5. Then, go to the Certification tab and Choose Birth or Death Certification.
  6. Enter Application Number and Search it, after searching, check your information before generating Certificates.
  7. If any Mistake with your Birth or Death Certificate, go to the Updation Tab and choose Birth or Death Updation.
  8. Use the Application to search your records and update your Birth or Death Certificates.
  9. In the Report Tab you can see all record of birth and death
